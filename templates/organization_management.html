About Scheduled Searches

This page allows you to configure automated searches of the GeM tender portal that will run at specified times.

  • Keyword - The search term to use on the GeM portal. Leave empty to browse all tenders.
  • Max Tenders - The maximum number of tenders to download and analyze.
  • Execution Time - The time when this search will run daily (24-hour format).

The system will automatically analyze the downloaded tenders for all organizations based on their service definitions.
